Welcome to Maze King!

Hit enter to start a maze and use the arrow keys to select one of the four doors (A, B, C, D) to proceed to the next room. Use the doors to get to the final room and escape. If you are stuggling to make it to the end 'click' on the solve button and follow the instructions to your escape! Note though that this will give you negative points.

Try to complete all seven mazes with the highest score!
